The purpose of a semi-structured interview is to gather additional information from the user that may not have been captured by geolocation or diary entries. This information will be used to supplement the study data and to investigate any relationship between participation (as measured by questionnaire) and the geolocation based summary measures collected from the participant's smartphone.

The session will provide an opportunity to discuss the participant's experience of the app, their perception of participating in the study and to reiterate how their data will be used. The WHODAS2.0 and GCPLA-r questionnaires will be conducted during this session before participants are discharged from the study. 

\subsection{Study Feedback}
The participant will be asked to provide feedback on the study, including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Do you feel your participation and activity over the data collection period was typical for you?
    \item In your own words, what was your overall experience of participating in the study? You may include positive and negative aspects of the study, as well as any suggestions for improvement in your answer.
    \item What was your experience/perception of having the types of locations you visited during this period recorded during this study?
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Would you change how the study was explained to you?
            \item How do you feel about the study now?
            \item Do you have any other comments or questions about the data collected during this study, or what is done with it?
        \end{itemize}
    \item What is your understanding of the data you have provided and how it will be used.
    \item Consider a set of entires, how did you acheive the activites contained in the entries? What was your aim? And did you participate with another person?

\end{itemize}
